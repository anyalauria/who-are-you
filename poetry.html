I

I was sitting on a hill
The sky was green
The grass was blue
The wind so still
I said to myself
Until

Until I know
What life is about
I’ll stay right here
Until it all becomes clear
Why the sour tarts tart
Why the frown horns horn
Why the iggle biggle wiggles
Why is meaning so fickle?

so I won’t move an inch
I won’t leave this place
I’ll watch the world spin around
And when it puts me in a daze
And my mind is completely crazed
I’ll shout and I’ll scream that It all makes sense
Why was I so tense?
